## COMMISSION CALCULATION QUICK GUIDE

### Step 1: Determine Performance Tier

- **Tier 1 (Rising Star):** 8-11 paid subs → 5% commission
- **Tier 2 (Achiever):** 12-16 paid subs → 7.5% commission
- **Tier 3 (Top Performer):** 17+ paid subs → 10% commission

### Step 2: Calculate Commission

```
Commission = Revenue × Commission Rate
```

### Step 3: Add Bonuses

```
Customer Bonus = Paid Subscriptions × ₦500
Yearly Plan Bonus = Yearly Subs × ₦1,000
Professional Plan Bonus = Pro Subs × ₦1,500
Monthly Target Bonus = ₦10,000 (if target hit)
Referral Bonus = Converting Referrals × ₦2,000
```

### Step 4: Total Payment

```
Total = Base Salary + Commission + All Bonuses
```

### Example (15 customers, ₦200,000 revenue)

- Tier 2 (12-16 subs): 7.5% commission
- Commission: ₦200,000 × 7.5% = ₦15,000
- Customer bonus: 15 × ₦500 = ₦7,500
- Plan bonuses: ~₦5,000 (varies)
- Target bonus: ₦10,000 (hit target)
- **Total bonuses: ~₦37,500**

## COMMON ISSUES & QUICK FIXES

### Issue: Low Response Rate

**Quick Fix:**

- Review messages - are they personalized?
- Try different times (morning vs evening)
- Focus on WhatsApp instead of DMs

### Issue: Demo No-Shows

**Quick Fix:**

- Send reminder 2 hours before
- Call 30 minutes before
- Shorten commitment (3 min instead of 5)

### Issue: Can't Convert Trials

**Quick Fix:**

- Day 1: Help them set up properly
- Day 3: Check-in call
- Day 7: Show ROI
- Day 15: Address objections
- Day 25: Create urgency

### Issue: Inconsistent Activity

**Quick Fix:**

- Daily morning check-in at 9 AM
- Pair with high performer
- Review schedule adherence
- Address motivation issues
