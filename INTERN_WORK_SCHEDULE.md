## UNDERSTANDING SELL SQUARE

### What is Sell Square?

Sell Square is a cloud-based inventory and business management platform that helps small and medium-sized businesses:

- Track inventory in real-time across multiple locations
- Manage sales with a built-in POS system
- Monitor staff activities and accountability
- Know exact profit margins on every sale
- Track unique products like gadgets with IMEI/serial numbers
- Manage customers and debtor payments

### Who Needs Sell Square?

**Primary Target Customers:**

1. Business owners using notebooks or Excel for inventory
2. Gadget/phone sellers (need IMEI tracking)
3. Fashion retailers (need variant management)
4. General retail stores with staff
5. Multi-location businesses
6. Anyone experiencing stock discrepancies

**Key Pain Points We Solve:**

- Manual inventory tracking errors
- Staff theft and accountability issues
- Not knowing real profit margins
- Stock count mismatches
- Lost sales records
- Unable to manage multiple locations

### Pricing Plans

**Free Plan**: Basic features, limited products
**Basic Plan**: 5,000 Naira/month or 50,000 Naira/year
**Standard Plan**: 10,000 Naira/month or 100,000 Naira/year
**Professional Plan**: 15,000 Naira/month or 150,000 Naira/year (includes IMEI tracking)

## COMMON OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

### "It's too expensive"

**Response:** "I understand. How much do you lose monthly to stock discrepancies and theft? Most businesses find Sell Square saves them money. Plus, we have a free plan to start with a 30-day trial of premium features."

### "I'm managing fine now"

**Response:** "That's great! Quick question - can you tell me your exact profit margin on each product right now? Can you see in real-time what's in stock across all locations? Sell Square makes these instant."

### "I'm not tech-savvy"

**Response:** "Perfect! If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Sell Square. We provide free setup and training. Let me show you how simple it is."

### "Let me think about it"

**Response:** "Absolutely! What specific concerns do you have? [Address them] How about we set you up on the free trial today - no payment needed. You can think while using it."
